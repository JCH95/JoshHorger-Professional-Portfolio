Scholarship Application

GitHub Repository


A website built for the Society of Martha Washington. Candidates who want to apply for the Washington DC Scholarship can view the trip details on this site, and also apply by filling out the application form, where the information is privately stored and sent to a panel of judges. The judges can grade the form online through the use of a gradig matrix designed using JavaScript. There is also a section to donate to The Society. This was built by a team of 6 developers within 2 weeks.






Languages

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind, jQuery

King Chess Ranking

GitHub Repository


This project is dedicated to a high school chess club. The goal of this project is to provide an app where the members of the chess club can track their ranking based on games they play within the chess club meetings. Once they create profiles and add all member to a bracket, they will play games against eachother, and the results will be tracked and added to the app by the head of the chess club. The app will feature a specific ELO algorithm to update their current standings amongst the other members. This app is still in development and was built by a team of 4 developers within 2 weeks.



Languages

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Handlebars, Shell, Node, Express, MySQL, Bcrypt

Life Sherpa

GitHub Repository


This project is a website where users may create an account and sign up for mentorship in a given list of topics. Once signed up, users can see a full list of the mentors on the site, as well as videos that mentors post to help teach mentees. Users can also choose to be a mentor on the site and post their own videos or pieces of wisdom they would like to share. This site is still in development, and hopes to add comments for videos, private communications between mentor and mentee, among other things. This was built by a team of 4 developers within a week and a half.
